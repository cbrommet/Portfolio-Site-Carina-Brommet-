My Story

I’m a UI/UX designer based in Northern California with a passion for blending creativity with functionality and accessibility to make products that can improve the lives of users. I have always been passionate about visual storytelling, receiving a bachelor’s degree in visual communication before I went on to earn a master’s in screenwriting for film and television. In the years since then, I have held a variety of roles, from copywriting to operations and administrative support. Now, I am blending my visual, creative background with my experience in logistics to pursue a career in UI/UX design.

I also still dabble in creative writing, especially in the podcast space, and in my free time enjoy reading, traveling, and playing video games.
