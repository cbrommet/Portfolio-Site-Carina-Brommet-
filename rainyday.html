RainyDay (Finance App)

RainyDay is a responsive web app with a mission to take the fear out of finance, allowing users to easily save for any life goal, big or small. Simply connect your bank, set up a goal, and the app will automatically save for you, based on your goal deadline.

Case Study

01 THE PROJECT

RainyDay is a comprehensive mobile app designed to help users easily save to reach their goals, big or small. With automated savings options and an extensive learning center, RainyDay is here to take the fear out of finance.

GOALS

02 THE USER

USER STORIES

Based on the brief, this app was to be geared towards users who are comfortable with technology, but don’t like managing their finances. The goal of the app was to both display user’s spending over time and provide them with information to help them save money within a certain amount of time.

03 THE BRAND

RainyDay is targeted towards users who want to make reaching their saving goals easy. A comprehensive set of brand guidelines was developed with the values of RainyDay at its core.

LOGO

The RainyDay logo features an umbrella protecting against rain, inspired by the mission to help people to save for more than just rainy days.

COLOR

RainyDay’s color palette of blue and purple is inspired by its rain theme. It gives the app a calm feel that can build trust and confidence in users

TYPOGRAPHY

Poppins is the chosen font of RainyDay and helps echo the casual, friendly feel that the app is built around.

ILLUSTRATION

RainyDay uses illustrations, rather than photography. These are simple, human-focused graphics related to finance or lifestyle, to highlight the mission of saving for life goals

06 TESTING

To improve the user experience of RainyDay, five potential users did a scenario test using the mid-fidelity prototype. Based on their feedback, I iterated and refined my designs to make the app more user friendly.

MAJOR CHANGES

07 FINAL DESIGN

ONBOARDING

Upon initial sign up, RainyDay walks a new user through linking their bank account, allowing for automated savings

HOMEPAGE

The central hub where users can see their spending over time, their current total cash, loans, credit card debt, the saving goals they’re closest to reaching and their recent transactions.

CREATING A GOAL

The user can view all of their ongoing savings goals from the saving homepage, as well as set up a new goal.

LEARNING CENTER

This is an educational hub accessed through the homepage where users can take courses and read tips on topics such as saving, budgeting, and investing.
